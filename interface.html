THE EXACT BRIDGE BETWEEN ERP AND POS

The Kenware ERP Interface allows you to automate the exchange of information, practically with whatever your ERP, and your Kenware Point of Sale as a single system, without requiring userintervention.

BENEFITS OF THE ERP KENWARE INTERFACE
